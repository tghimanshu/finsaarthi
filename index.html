What We Do

Finsaarthi envisions making the most populous nation of the world with a thriving human capital a financially informed and empowered one.

To create financial wellness among individuals and families with updated, accurate and legitimate knowledge through continuous financial education and investor awareness programs

To design and simplify financial education programs which will help everyone from new and young investors to retiring seasoned veteran strategically direct their own finance and take appropriate financial decisions and therefore achieve ultimate financial independence.

We are committed to full disclosure of facts and the truth in all dealings with external and internal stakeholders and responsible for all our action towards the organisation
